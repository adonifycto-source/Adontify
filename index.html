The problem

Chapter management is broken

Most chapters are run on group chats and spreadsheets. It works until it doesn't.

No visibility into participation

Officers have no easy way to see who's keeping up with requirements and who's falling behind until it's too late.

Hours spent on admin work

Tracking points, approving submissions, and chasing down dues takes hours every week — time that should be spent on the chapter.

Members don't know where they stand

Without a clear view of their own progress, members stay disengaged and officers are left guessing who needs a nudge.

Dues and finances are a mess

Collecting dues through Venmo requests and tracking who's paid in a Google Sheet is unreliable and embarrassing at the end of the semester.

How it works

Set up in an afternoon

No technical setup required. Add your members, create your tasks, and you're running.

01

Add your chapter

Create your organization, add your members, and configure your point requirements for the semester. Takes about 10 minutes.

02

Create tasks and goals

Build out your task list. Assign point values, set due dates, and tie tasks to semester goals like community service hours.

03

Members apply and earn points

Members submit proof of task completion. Officers approve with one click — points are added automatically.

04

Everyone sees the leaderboard

A live leaderboard keeps members engaged and accountable. At-risk members are flagged automatically so officers can reach out early.

Features

Everything your chapter needs

Built specifically for the way Greek chapters and student orgs actually operate.

Points & Leaderboard

Assign points to any task or event. A live leaderboard makes participation competitive and transparent for everyone in the chapter.

Task Management

Create tasks with point values, due dates, and categories. Members submit completion proof and officers approve in one click.

Goal Tracking

Set semester-wide goals — community service hours, event attendance — and watch progress update automatically as tasks are approved.

At-Risk Alerts

Members falling behind on point requirements are flagged automatically so you can reach out before they're in violation.

Dues & Finances

Track who has paid dues, manage committee budgets, and see your chapter's financial picture without a spreadsheet.

Member View

Every member gets their own view of their points, pending tasks, and standing in the chapter — no more "what do I still need to do?"
